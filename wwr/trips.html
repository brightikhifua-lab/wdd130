Our Featured Expeditions

The Salmon River Splash

Duration: Half-Day (3 Hours) | Difficulty: Class II-III

The absolute ideal trip for families, beginners, and group parties looking for a balanced mix of fun introductory rapids and stunning mountain canyon scenery. Includes high-quality safety gear and snacks.

Titan Canyon Extreme

Duration: Full-Day (6 Hours) | Difficulty: Class IV

Prepare to get soaked! This action-packed route takes you directly through deep, tight gorges featuring continuous technical drops and high-energy wave trains. A catered riverside deli lunch is provided halfway through.

Wilderness Retreat

Duration: 3 Days / 2 Nights | Difficulty: Class III-IV

Disconnect from the digital world completely. Tackle over 45 unique rapids by day and unwind on white-sand river beaches by night. Our guides manage all camp setup, gourmet campfire cooking, and safety operations.
